Today is Friday, April 7th, 2017. I now writing my diary, the time is 18:16, the weather is a little bit cool. When I am reading, speaking, listening and writing, I don't have much confidence in my English. I really don't know how to start, because my English grammar is very poor. Somebody told me: "If you want to study any languages, you must start from the beginning, that is write your 'daily diary". At first time, I didn't believe what he said, but I want to check. I want to see if this way is right or wrong. If I follow his suggestion and I can improve my English, I will just have to believe him! If anyone knows the way to start to learn English, help me, please! Bobby.

White Space Everywhere.

  • April 6, 2017
    • •
  • Joomla, Drupal
    • •
  • Bobbt

Today is Thursday, April 6, 2017. I wake up on the bed at 4 o’clock in the morning. When I am awake I do not get up immediately. I am continuing to check my mindfulness (Sati). The way to check is to be aware of what are you doing at each moment. For example: standing, knowing standing; walking, knowing walking; sitting, knowing sitting; lying, knowing lying; closing my eyes, knowing closing my eyes; opening my eyes, knowing opening my eyes; turning to lie on my left side, knowing turning to lie on left side; turning onto my right side, knowing turning on to my right side; moving my finger, knowing moving my finger; moving my hand, knowing moving my hand; feeling an itch, knowing feeling an itch, feeling hot, knowing feeling hot; thinking, knowing thinking, etc. But the most important is to tell our mind, every time our body moves, or our mind moves, it is not really you moving your body, or moving your mind, it is no more than thinking like a moving machine. Why am I practising this way? Because, I want to train my mind to become strong! Actually, many of people don’t know what are they doing at any present moment. I don’t want to talk a lot about this, I will bore my readers. After I leave my bedroom, I prepare to defecate and to urinate, and then to take a bath. I'm so sorry to tell about this matter, probably there will be people who cannot accept it, but I have to write it down, because this is the real story, even though some people might think I have no manners anymore. It takes me about 30 minutes for this entire activity. After that I prepare for morning chanting and sitting meditation. Today I have no classes, so I have more time to practice my English, and to prepare for my driver's license test on Tuesday. I have the whole day to spend doing that. There are two kinds of test: written and practical. I have to finish my article now. I still have many stories to tell you all. See you next time. Bobby

My life seems to consist mainly of rowing or coaching outings at fairly regular intervals during the week, requiring some self discipline in terms of bed times, which it took a while to admit (slept through term 1’s lectures in the first year – not the best plan!). Returning home in the morning I soak luxuriously in a hot tub for quarter of an hour or more, having no lectures before 10am . Then a couple of lectures, or maybe a practical, and maybe even a supervision, all muddled into the hours before 7pm. It sometimes feels like the “structured teaching” breaks up my work schedule, but they’re important and useful, so I do make the effort to go.
